Community Bulletin

The city council has announced a new green initiative this month. Residents are encouraged to plant at least one tree in their neighbourhood before end of season.

The annual food festival will feature over 50 stalls this year, showcasing cuisines from across the region. Entry is open to all residents and their families.

Sports registrations are now open for the inter-ward tournament. Teams of up to 11 members can register at the community centre or online through this portal.

The health camp in May will offer free checkups and blood tests. No prior appointment is needed. Visiting doctors from the city hospital will be present.
